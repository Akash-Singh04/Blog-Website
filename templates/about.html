Get To Know More About Me!

To be young and to be free is something we all crave. The feeling I tend to strive for is to be independent, To not be answerable to someone when i want to sleep in the library for hours,To not have to ask for permission to have an extra cup of chai in the evening.

In all honesty,Music means a lot to me as a person.I associate places,people,memories with songs. If i hear Somebody To You By The Vamps,I slip into the streets of Delhi while When We Feel Young By When Chai Meets Toast literally sends chills down my spine as i am thrown back to the pretty hills of kashmir. Talk about being a dramatic ass but thats just me,A person made up of lame jokes and refrences from shows that literally no one has watched.

Winters,Hoodies,Indian LOFI-Sums up the best time of the year for me.Fears? None other than the fact that i may not live upto the hype I've set for my life inside my head.

Is it too far for a small town boy from a middle class family to dream to go to L.A. someday?Come the day buddy,We shall see.Come the day~
